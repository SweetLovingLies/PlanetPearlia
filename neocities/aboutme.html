I have had a love for coding and all things design since as long as I can remember.

I found my creative outlet being early DeviantArt, Toyhou.se, and Amino, but I ended up moving away from a lot of those options as I grew up. I'm pretty much off the social media grid now, but I do intend to begin content creation on IG and Youtube sometime in the near future. After discovering Neocities in 2021, I fell in love and planned out multiple different website designs heavily inspired by sites like Cinni.net and Comfort.

Programming for me is entirely about self-expression and it always will be. Although I do program commercially now, I do believe that the internet is for the people, rather than for large companies! I'm a forever believer in the indie web.

I am currently a student at the Apple Developer Academy, and after I graduate, I intend to go back into the traditional college system for graphic design and web development. Lots of ambition!
